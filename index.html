Uber
Eats

Uber Eats is a global leader in the food delivery industry, revolutionizing the way people order and enjoy food. Launched by Uber in 2014, Uber Eats connects customers with local restaurants, offering a wide variety of food options delivered directly to their doorsteps. Our mission is to make food delivery faster, more convenient, and accessible to everyone, whether you're craving a quick snack or a full meal. With a focus on innovation, we’ve created a platform that not only makes it easy for customers to order meals but also helps local businesses grow by providing them with the tools and technology they need to succeed in the competitive food delivery market. We operate in over 6,000 cities across 45 countries, making it easier for people around the world to access their favorite meals at the touch of a button. At Uber Eats, we’re dedicated to creating a seamless experience for both customers and delivery partners by offering reliable and fast service. Whether it’s through our app's user-friendly interface, AI-powered features like the menu reader, or our commitment to supporting local eateries, we work hard to make every meal a satisfying experience. We’re not just about food—we’re about bringing people together and making cities more connected. As part of Uber's broader mission to help people go anywhere and get anything, Uber Eats is committed to sustainability and working toward a future where all deliveries are made using electric vehicles, reducing emissions and making our world a cleaner place. With a focus on technology, convenience, and customer satisfaction, Uber Eats continues to set the standard for food delivery services worldwide.
